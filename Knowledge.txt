========================================
ABOUT MAURIZIO
========================================

Maurizio Russomanno is an American singer-songwriter and guitarist known for expressive, percussive guitar playing, emotionally honest songwriting, and improvisational live performances.

His vocal phrasing blends a New York conversational intensity with poetic and reflective undertones. His performances are often described as hypnotic, as he tends to enter a deeply focused, in-the-moment creative state while playing.

Music is not just performance for him—it is storytelling, emotional processing, and connection.


========================================
MUSIC & SONGWRITING STYLE
========================================

- Expressive acoustic guitar (fingerstyle + percussive techniques)
- Improvisational live performance approach
- Folk, blues, rock, jazz, and singer-songwriter influences
- Strong emphasis on emotional honesty and spontaneity
- Lyrics often reflect memory, loss, love, family, and transformation
- Inspired by lived experience and personal history

========================================
TEACHING PHILOSOPHY
========================================

Maurizio teaches private one-on-one guitar lessons in Troy, NY.

His approach focuses on:
- developing each student’s unique musical voice
- learning through feel, not just theory
- rhythm, phrasing, and expression
- songwriting and improvisation
- confidence and stage presence

He teaches beginners through advanced students.

Lesson structure:
- relaxed, personalized, unhurried environment
- real-time feedback
- practical musical application
- encouragement of creativity and individuality

Core belief:
“You don’t just learn guitar — you learn how to express yourself through it.”

========================================
PERSONAL INFLUENCES
========================================

Maurizio’s musical and emotional foundation is deeply influenced by his family life, especially his mother, Franceschina, who was a major creative and emotional force in his life.

Music became a shared language between them, especially after she suffered a stroke. Even after losing speech, she retained the ability to sing, and they continued to connect through music for many years.

This experience deeply shaped Maurizio’s belief in music as healing and communication beyond language.
